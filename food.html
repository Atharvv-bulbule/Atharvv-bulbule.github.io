Famous Food of Solapur

Jowar Bhakri

Jowar Bhakri is a traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour. It is commonly served with spicy curries, vegetables, and chutneys in the Solapur region.

Shenga Chutney

Shenga Chutney is a dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices. It is usually eaten with bhakri or chapati.

Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a famous Maharashtrian street food made from spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread. It is popular in Solapur and nearby cities.

Pilgrimage Town Foods

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils. It is commonly prepared during festivals and religious celebrations.

Sabudana Khichdi

Sabudana Khichdi is a popular fasting dish made with tapioca pearls, peanuts, and spices. It is widely eaten by pilgrims visiting Pandharpur and Tuljapur during religious fasting days.
