Food of Solapur

Authentic Maharashtrian flavors, traditional dishes, and vibrant street food

Famous Dishes

Solapur offers a variety of traditional Maharashtrian dishes that reflect the local culture and flavors

🫓

Jowar Bhakri

A traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour. It is commonly served with spicy curries, chutneys, and vegetables. Thick, wholesome, and deeply satisfying.

🥜

Shenga Chutney

A dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices. It is often eaten with bhakri or chapati and is a beloved accompaniment in every Solapur household.

🌶️

Misal Pav

A popular Maharashtrian street food made with spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread. One of the most iconic dishes of the region, famous for its bold flavors and fiery spice.

🍬

Puran Poli

A sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils. It is usually prepared during festivals and special occasions, and is a cherished traditional delicacy loved by all.
