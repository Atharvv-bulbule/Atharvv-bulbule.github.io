Important Places

Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar. The temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake and attracts thousands of devotees every day.

Pandharpur

Located about 70 km from Solapur, Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple and the Wari pilgrimage.

Akkalkot

A spiritual destination located 40 km from Solapur known for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj.

Solapur Fort

Also called Bhuikot Fort, this historic monument reflects medieval military architecture and represents Solapur's rich history.
