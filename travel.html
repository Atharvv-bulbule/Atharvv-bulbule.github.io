Travel Guide

Everything you need to know to reach Solapur and get around

How to Reach Solapur

Solapur is well connected to major cities across India

🛣️ By Road

Solapur is well connected by highways to major cities such as Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. Regular government and private buses operate between these cities.

Distance from Major Cities
  • Pune 250 km
  • Hyderabad 300 km
  • Bengaluru 380 km
  • Mumbai 400 km

🚂 By Rail

Solapur Railway Station is a major railway junction. Many trains connect Solapur to cities like Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru. Rail travel is one of the most convenient ways to reach the city.

✦ Solapur Railway Station is a major junction with frequent trains to all major Indian cities.

✈️ By Air

Solapur has a small airport but limited flight services. Travelers can reach Solapur from the nearest major airports by road or train.

Nearest Major Airports
  • Pune International Airport ~250 km
  • Hyderabad International Airport ~300 km

🛺 Local Transport

Visitors can move around the city using a variety of local transport options. Nearby pilgrimage towns like Pandharpur and Akkalkot are easily accessible by road.

Available Options
  • Auto Rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City Buses
💡

Traveler's Tip

Solapur serves as a gateway to several important pilgrimage destinations. If you plan to visit Pandharpur and Akkalkot, it is best to hire a private taxi or take a direct bus from Solapur city center. Local transport is affordable and readily available throughout the day.
