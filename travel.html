From Solapur to Nearby Destinations

Pandharpur, Tuljapur, and Akkalkot — all within easy reach

🛕

Solapur → Pandharpur

~70 km~1.5 hrs

🛣️ By Road (Bus)

MSRTC (Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation) runs regular buses from Solapur Bus Stand to Pandharpur. Buses are available throughout the day and are the most popular and affordable option for pilgrims.

Key Info
  • Departure Point Solapur Central Bus Stand
  • Travel Time ~1.5 to 2 hrs
  • Frequency Every 30–60 min
  • Fare Approx. ₹80–₹120
