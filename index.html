Welcome to Solapur

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra, close to the borders of Karnataka and Telangana. The city is known for its spiritual centers, historic monuments, traditional festivals, and vibrant culture.

For centuries, Solapur has been an important destination for pilgrims and travelers. Famous spiritual places such as Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur, and Akkalkot are located in and around the city.

Solapur is also known for its textile industry, especially Solapur chaddars and towels which are famous across India.

Visitors can experience spirituality, history, traditions, and delicious regional cuisine while exploring this beautiful city.
